Reception, no longer a hassle

inline is a booking management system designed exclusively for helping restaurant owners to improve efficiency and reduce errors.

Restaurants around the world have chosen inline.
Powerful Productivity Tools
Increase booking efficiency with these key features

Booking management

Information compiled and displayed in suitable formats

Quickly browse through daily booking lists, table maps, and guest information. View bookings made from all platforms at a glance.

Automated booking confirmation

inline will send a text message to confirm the booking before the time of dining to reduce manual errors and make booking management more efficient.

Set available booking times

Configure table maps and VIP tables according to your current floor plan and more. Set the number of available bookings for each mealtime and never worry about over-booking.

Manage deposits

Have your guests put down a deposit and pay online by credit card before dining. Easily track the status of the payments on your iPad in real-time.

Cloud management

The powerful cloud system updates data and syncs it across multiple platforms in real-time. Managing bookings is much more convenient.

Queuing Management

Multiple ways to queue

Let guests add themselves to the queue on an iPad or a kiosk. Guests can also check-in with a QR code and then check their queuing status on the TV.

Queue number assignment

Guests are assigned queue numbers based on when they got in the queue.

No app required

Guests will receive a link via SMS to check their queuing status in a web browser. There’s no need for guests to waste time on downloading an app to check their status.

One tap to call guests

Call the next party in the queue with one tap on the iPad. Serve guests more efficiently by saving time on manually inputting phone numbers.

Monitor your bookings and queue remotely

Even if you’re not physically present, you can remotely monitor booking and queuing statuses with a mobile phone that can connect to the internet.

Guest Management

Guest database

View dining history and information of guests across all restaurants you manage. Each booking is saved to help you build your guest database.

Record guest preferences

One guest doesn’t eat spicy food? Another prefers to sit outdoors? Record whatever notes you see fit in inline and show the guests you care when they return next time

Valuable data and tools

Use marketing, data acquisition, and reporting tools to help your staff manage their relationships with guests. They will love you more and return frequently!
